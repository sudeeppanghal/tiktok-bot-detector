Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Instagram Campaign Verifier app processes public Instagram profile metadata and post metrics (followers, likes, comments, post counts) provided directly by creators or fetched via public Meta Graph API endpoints to verify campaign authenticity.

2. How We Use Information

We use this data exclusively to calculate engagement rates, detect automated bot activities, and verify campaign compliance for master admins.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

We do not sell, store private passwords, or share personal user data with third parties. All analytics are used solely for campaign verification.
