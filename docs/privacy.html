Campaign Analytics & Authenticity Verification

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Summary: TikTok Campaign Tracker only accesses TikTok account data that users explicitly authorize via TikTok Login. We never sell, share, or misuse your data. Data is used solely to verify authentic engagement for campaign managers.

1. Who We Are

TikTok Campaign Tracker ("the App") is developed and operated by @batmanjaatwhop. The App is a campaign analytics tool that helps campaign managers verify authentic engagement from creators who have joined their campaigns.

Contact: t.me/batmanjaatwhop

2. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you explicitly connect your TikTok account through the official TikTok Login Kit (OAuth). This includes:

  • TikTok Username & Display Name — To identify your account in the admin dashboard
  • Profile Statistics — Follower count, following count, total likes, video count
  • Video Analytics — Per-video view counts, like counts, comments, shares
  • Engagement Metrics — Watch time, completion rates (when authorized)
  • Audience Demographics — Country breakdown, age/gender distribution (when authorized)
  • Traffic Sources — How viewers found your content (FYP, Search, etc.) (when authorized)

We do NOT collect: passwords, payment information, private messages, or any data beyond what TikTok's API explicitly provides with your permission.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your TikTok data is used exclusively for:

  • Verifying authentic engagement on campaign-related content
  • Detecting artificial/bot-driven view inflation to protect campaign integrity
  • Displaying analytics to authorized campaign administrators
  • Generating authenticity scores based on real engagement ratios

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of campaign verification.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties except:

  • Campaign Administrators: The admin of the campaign you joined may see your analytics to verify engagement authenticity
  • TikTok: Data is retrieved directly from TikTok's official API — TikTok's own Privacy Policy applies

5. Data Storage & Retention

Analytics data is stored locally on the administrator's device and/or in a secure private database accessible only to authorized campaign administrators. We retain your data only for the duration of the campaign unless you request deletion.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via Telegram: @batmanjaatwhop

6. TikTok Login & Permissions

When you connect your TikTok account, we request the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic — Basic profile information
  • user.info.stats — Follower and engagement statistics
  • video.list — List of your public videos and their metrics

You can revoke access at any time through TikTok's app: Settings → Privacy → Apps & Websites

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted token storage and HTTPS-only data transmission. Access to admin analytics is protected by secure authentication.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
